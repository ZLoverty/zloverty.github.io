Choosing appropriate time interval for calculating concentration difference

I chose 10 frames as the time interval for calculating concentration difference in the previous post Intensity autocorrelation. After a quick test, I found 10 frames may not be a good choice, because the subtraction field does not show any noticeable feature (see below).

So I am doing an experiment to see at what time interval features will come out.

Comments

The highest correlation between \(\nabla\cdot(c\boldsymbol{v})\) and $\Delta c$ is 0.02. This is on the same order of the noise in the concentration time series data. Therefore, there is no appropriate time interval, and no correlation can be claimed between the \(\nabla\cdot(c\boldsymbol{v})\) and \(\Delta c\).
